# Director of Development & Capital Partnerships

## The Opportunity

Queen Ventures is seeking an experienced **capital partner and deal operator** to lead the structuring and execution of a multi-market capital strategy across our affordable housing and workforce development platform. This is not a traditional nonprofit role—it's a high-autonomy, deal-focused opportunity with significant upside participation.

You'll work alongside AI-driven systems to scale our impact from Georgia and Hawaii into a $21M Texas expansion, building the capital infrastructure for a national platform that combines real estate, technology training, and pathways to homeownership for veterans and foster youth.

---

## About Queen Ventures

Queen Ventures is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit community development association transforming lives through an integrated model of stable housing, technology training, and pathways to financial sovereignty.

### Our Platform

**Georgia (Operational Hub)**
- Organizational base and strategic partnerships
- Capital and development pipeline management

**Hawaii (Active Program Site)**
- Oceanfront multi-unit property generating revenue
- Mission Forward Initiative: supportive housing + AI/cloud vocational training + workforce partnerships
- Capital optimization opportunities: refinancing, rental/student housing expansion (near BYU–Hawaii)

**Texas (Expansion Market)**
- $21M acquisition of 54 condominium units
- Scalable model for national expansion

### Our Impact

Through the Mission Forward Initiative, we provide:
- Safe, stable housing for foster youth aging out of care and transitioning veterans
- Industry-recognized AI and cloud computing training
- Paid consulting projects ($8K–$12K earnings during 180-day program)
- Job placement pathways ($60K–$100K+ salaries)
- Seed funding opportunities (up to $25K)
- Homeownership preparation and qualification

## The Role: Director of Development & Capital Partnerships

### Core Responsibilities

**Capital Strategy & Structuring**
- Design and execute a blended capital strategy across multiple markets
- Structure deals combining CDFI debt, bank financing (CRA, AHP), government grants, and impact/Opportunity Zone equity
- Build and manage a pipeline of funding opportunities ($5M–$50M+)

**Relationship Development & Execution**
- Lead relationships with CDFIs (Enterprise, LISC, Clearinghouse, LIIF)
- Cultivate partnerships with banks (CRA, FHLB AHP participants)
- Navigate government agencies (HUD, HOME, CDBG programs)
- Engage foundations and impact investors
- Negotiate and close financing (debt, grants, equity)

**Market Integration**
- Integrate Georgia, Hawaii, and Texas operations into a unified capital strategy
- Identify refinancing and capital extraction opportunities
- Optimize asset utilization across all markets

## Required Experience

- **Proven track record** raising $5M–$50M+ in capital
- **Deep expertise** in affordable housing, community development, or structured finance
- **Strong familiarity** with:
  - CDFI landscape and relationships
  - HUD, HOME, CDBG programs
  - Blended capital structures
  - Deal closing (not just sourcing)
- **Established network** of funders, lenders, and institutional partners
