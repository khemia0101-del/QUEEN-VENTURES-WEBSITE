# Director of Development & Capital Partnerships
## Capital Partner Role at Queen Ventures

---

## 🎯 The Opportunity

Lead capital strategy across a multi-market affordable housing and workforce development platform. Structure blended capital combining CDFI debt, bank financing, government grants, and impact equity. High autonomy, deal-focused, significant upside.

**This is not a traditional nonprofit role.**

---

## 📍 Our Platform

| Market | Focus | Opportunity |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Georgia** | Operational Hub | Strategic partnerships & capital pipeline |
| **Hawaii** | Active Program | Mission Forward Initiative + refinancing opportunities |
| **Texas** | Expansion | $21M acquisition of 54 units – scalable model |

---

## 💼 What You'll Do

- Design and execute blended capital strategy across multiple markets
- Lead relationships with CDFIs, banks, government agencies, and impact investors
- Negotiate and close financing (debt, grants, equity)
- Build and manage a $5M–$50M+ funding pipeline
- Integrate multi-market operations into unified capital strategy

---

## ✅ What We're Looking For

**Required Experience:**
- Proven track record raising $5M–$50M+ in capital
- Deep expertise in affordable housing, community development, or structured finance
- Strong familiarity with CDFIs, HUD/HOME/CDBG programs, blended capital structures
- Established network of funders, lenders, and institutional partners
- Ability to close capital—not just source it

**Who You Are:**
- Capital raiser, not administrator
- Operator who wants deal-level upside
- Comfortable with high autonomy + high accountability
- Open to leveraging AI to increase efficiency and scale

---

## 💰 Compensation

Performance-based with significant upside:

- **Success Fee:** 1%–3% of all capital raised
- **Milestone Bonuses:** Paid at key funding thresholds
- **Deferred Compensation:** $100K+ equivalent paid upon funding/refinance
- **Profit Participation:** 5%–10% of project-level upside
- **Long-term leadership opportunity** as the platform scales

---

## 🤖 AI-First Platform

Queen Ventures operates with AI-driven systems for grant writing, pipeline tracking, and outreach. You don't need to be an AI expert—you need to be comfortable learning quickly, working alongside automation, and directing AI outputs to increase efficiency and scale.

---

## 📍 Location & Travel

- **Primary:** Remote (with flexibility)
- **Travel:** Quarterly/as-needed to Georgia, Hawaii, and Texas

## 🚀 About Queen Ventures

We're transforming lives through an integrated model combining:

- Pathways to financial sovereignty and homeownership
